About Billionaire Picks

Our Story

Billionaire Picks began with a bold vision — to offer free, intelligent soccer betting insights to those who take betting seriously. Founded in Soweto by three brothers with a shared love for the game, we’ve combined years of experience with data-driven strategies to create a platform that prioritizes quality, honesty, and consistency. Our community is built on trust, and our picks are backed by research — not luck.

Meet SSM

SSM stands for the three founders behind Billionaire Picks. As brothers, we each bring a unique mindset and skill set — from analytics to match analysis to bankroll strategy. Together, we represent a united front for bettors who demand excellence and results. Billionaire Picks isn’t just a brand — it’s a lifestyle built around precision, discipline, and the hunger to win smart.
