About Velor

Velor is a thrift clothing brand started in Soweto by Thato Selebano. The brand focuses on bringing affordable, stylish, and high-quality fashion to young people while promoting sustainability and creativity.

Our Goal

Our goal is to empower youth through fashion, encourage individuality, and make premium clothing accessible without compromising on quality.
