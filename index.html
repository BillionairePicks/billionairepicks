Business goal

StatStake's mission is to become the go-to, student-driven analytics resource for football fans across local and international leagues. Over the next 18 months we aim to:

  • Deliver clear, data-driven match summaries and daily predictions that are easy to use.
  • Grow a small, sustainable community by partnering with local student clubs and streaming results on low-cost hosting.
  • Train other student contributors in data collection and responsible analytics so we can scale the product while keeping costs low.
